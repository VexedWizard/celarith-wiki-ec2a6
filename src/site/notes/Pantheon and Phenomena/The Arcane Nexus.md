Once the logistical lifeblood of Celarith, this arcane network of teleportation circles allowed for frictionless travel between Celarith's continents and regions for those who were magically inclined, or had the coin or influence to purchase Nexus Medallions, magically imbued teleportation aids. The Nexus was closed a decade ago in order to stop mages from making quick exits when trying to evade justice.  However, there have been recent calls to reopen it, especially from [[Regions and NPCS/Valedorn/Sister Ivara Cael- Voice of the Emberwardens\|Sister Ivara Cael- Voice of the Emberwardens]].
