### 1. **Varnir, the Shattering Sun**

**Domains:** Light, Creation, and Destruction  
**Symbol:** A half-shattered sun  
### 2. **Sylara, the Whispering Breeze**

**Domains:** Knowledge, Ignorance, Secrets 
**Symbol:** A swirling gust with faint glowing runes inside, obscured by mist.  
### 3. **Nyxira, the Silent Song**

**Domains:** Darkness, Dreams, and Protection  
**Symbol:** A crescent moon overlaid with an eye and three stars beneath it.  
### 4. **Golmor, the Iron Warden**

**Domains:** Law, War, and Vengeance  
**Symbol:** A broken shield with a spear through it.  
### 5. **Teralis, the Bittersweet Harvest**

**Domains:** Agriculture, Seasons, and Sacrifice  
**Symbol:** A wilted plant wrapped in thorns, with new buds beginning to grow from the roots.  
### 6. **Erythos, the Veiled Serpent**

**Domains:** Wisdom, Time, Deceit 
**Symbol:** A serpent coiled around an hourglass, its tail obscured in shadow.  
### 7. Kaelos, the Echoing Forge:

**Domains:** Craftsmanship, Artifice, and Memory
**Symbol:** A shining hammer, resting on a weathered anvil.
### 8. Allyra, the Shifting Tides:

**Domains:** The Seas, Emotions, and Uncertainty
**Symbol:** A tsunami crashing into an abyss
### 9. Chronomos, the Unbound Arcane:

**Domains:** Magic, Order, and Chaos.
**Symbol:** A swirling vortex of arcane energy, contained within a sphere of fractured crystal.
